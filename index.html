Math Minigame Factor Catcher

Factors, multiples, primes & more — catch the right numbers before they fall past!


How to Play

  • 🎯
    Each round shows a rule — e.g. "Catch multiples of 3".
  • 🔢
    Numbered orbs fall from the top. Green orbs match the rule; red orbs do not — but you must decide!
  • 🕹️
    Move the collector with ← → / A D keys or drag on the screen.
  • ✅
    Catch correct numbers: +10 points. Catch wrong ones: −1 life.
  • 💀
    You have 3 lives. Lose them all and the run ends immediately.
  • 🏆
    Survive 10 rounds (15 sec each) and earn up to 3 stars!
